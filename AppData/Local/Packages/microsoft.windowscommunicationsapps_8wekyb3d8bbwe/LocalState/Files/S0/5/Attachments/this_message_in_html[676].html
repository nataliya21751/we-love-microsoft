About Us

As a comprehensive health science university, the mission of The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston is to educate health science professionals, discover and translate advances in the biomedical and social sciences, and model the best practices in clinical care and public health.

Learn More

Culture

The University of Texas Health Science Center Houston (UTHealth) strives to create a culture in which faculty, staff and students are able to correlate how their work contributes to the mission of the university through recognition of their efforts. UTHealth is also committed to creating an institutional culture that fosters and celebrates diversity and inclusion. This is accomplished through internal recognition programs as well as outreach programs throughout the community.

Learn More

Pay and Benefits

The University of Texas Health Science Center Houston (UTHealth) offers competitive salaries designed to reward education, experience and excellent performance as well as a first class benefits package that includes medical, dental and life insurance plans, retirement plan options, tuition program, generous leave programs and more!

Learn More

Worklife Balance

The University of Texas Health Science Center Houston (UTHealth) believes in a balance between all aspects of life such as work, family, and health. UTHealth offers several benefits to assist employees with this balance such as a robust Employee Assistance Program (EAP) and several other partnerships that are resources for UTHealth employees. Additionally, UTHealth promotes a collaborative working environment that encourages creativity, innovation and excellence in the workplace.

Learn More

Job Enhancement & Advancement

The University of Texas Health Science Center Houston (UTHealth) is an organization that is dedicated to providing a high level of job security for its employees, as evident by the average tenure of employment being seven and a half years. Employees are given the opportunity to grow professionally and are able to develop themselves into a talented, engaged and driven workforce through favorable programs provided at UTHealth and a culture that encourages advancement from within.
